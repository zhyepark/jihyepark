About me

Hi, I’m Jihye.

I’m a UX and Product Designer with a background in fashion design and merchandising. My previous work in market research, trend analysis, customer insights, and brand positioning taught me how to recognize patterns, understand changing needs, and translate research into product decisions.

I’m currently pursuing an M.S. in Human-Centered Computing at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County. As a Graduate Research Assistant, I support qualitative research focused on digital mental health technologies, including participant recruitment and semi-structured interviews.

I also contribute UX research and accessibility insights to a nonprofit website redesign through a university-sponsored collaboration with Southwest Emergency Services. These experiences are helping me deepen my practice in user-centered research, inclusive design, and community-focused technology.

I’m passionate about creating intuitive and engaging digital experiences that balance human needs, thoughtful visual design, and real-world product goals. I approach this transition with curiosity, care, and a commitment to continuous learning.
